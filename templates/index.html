Basil Swimmer

Basil Andrew Swimmer - דב סוימר
Born 27.10.1941 South Africa and made Aliya to Israel in 1977.
The son of Rebecka and Avraham Swimmer, and the middle brother of Lennie and Courine (blessed memory)
Married to Phyllis, a father to Aviva, blessed memory, Osnat and Cherilee, and is the happiest grandfather to 11 wonderful grandchildren. (Moran, Ziv, Shira, Shai, Michal, Guy, Yotam, Eran, Dror, Yoav and Avner)

Basil has been painting and drawing in oil paints, water colours, acrylic, chalk ect.
His paintings are a world of nature, feelings, imaginary and thoughts.
Each painting can express so many feelings and beauty. His paintings include scenery from all over the world: South Africa, Israel, Switzerland, Netherlands and more.
